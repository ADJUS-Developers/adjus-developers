Our Projects

Take a look at some of our successful projects and clients.

PaySheet

Electronic PaySheet is a web-based software program seamlessly integrated with the E - Pay Wallet mobile wallet system. It allows companies to process employee payments automatically with just a single button click. This solution streamlines payroll management, ensuring secure and efficient payment transfers to employees' mobile wallet accounts, reducing manual intervention and ensuring timely, accurate payments.

E - Pay Wallet

E-Pay Wallet is a mobile wallet system integrated with the Electronic PaySheet, designed to handle various financial transactions like mobile payments through the PaySheet web application.
The system also connects with the E-Pay Wallet mobile app, allowing users to access their accounts for cash transfers, withdrawals, bill payments, and forex trading.

Explore

E - Pay Wallet Mobile App

E - Pay Wallet Mobile App enables employees to receive payments directly into their wallet accounts. This secure and user-friendly app allows users to manage their digital wallet, supporting mobile payments, money transfers, bill payments, cash withdrawals, and forex trading. Integrated with the Electronic PaySheet system, it automates employee payments, providing a seamless financial experience.
